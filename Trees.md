Some of my least favorite LeetCode questions, although realistically some of the most useful.

Kind of how all squares are rectangles but not all rectangles are squares, all trees are graphs but not all graphs are trees. Following are some characteristics of trees:
- They **cannot contain cycles**
- There must be at most **one path between any two nodes**
- Presence of a **root** (node who is "parent" to all nodes under it)
